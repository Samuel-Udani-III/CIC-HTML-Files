Refunds & Returns

We want you to be happy with your purchase! If you're not fully satisfied, we offer a hassle-free return and refund process. Please read the following guidelines:

Returns:

Eligibility: You can return items within 30 days of receiving your order.

Condition: Items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.

Non-returnable items: Sale items, gift cards, and personalized products cannot be returned.

How to Initiate a Return:

Contact our customer support team at support@yourstore.com to request a return.

Provide your order number and the item(s) you wish to return.

We will send you instructions for returning the product.

Refunds:

Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned item.

Method of Refund: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Shipping Costs: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or damaged.

Damaged or Defective Items:

If you received a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@yourstore.com with your order number and photos of the damage. We will cover return shipping costs and send a replacement or issue a refund.

Exchanges:

We do not offer direct exchanges, but you may return the item and place a new order for the desired product.

For any questions, please feel free to contact us at support@yourstore.com.
