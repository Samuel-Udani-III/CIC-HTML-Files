Frequently Asked Questions

How to place an order with USO

Placing an order with USO is simple:

  • Browse Our Products/Services – Visit our website and explore our offerings.
  • Select Your Items/Service – Choose the products or services you need and add them to your cart or request a quote.
  • Checkout & Payment – Proceed to checkout, fill in your details, and complete the payment process. We accept [list payment methods].
  • Order Confirmation – Once your order is placed, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your order details.
  • Shipping & Delivery – Your order will be processed and shipped according to our standard delivery times. You’ll receive tracking information if applicable.
  • For any questions or assistance, feel free to contact our support team at info@usodeals.com.

Why am I unable to place an order?

If you're having trouble placing an order with USO, here are a few possible reasons and solutions:

  • Payment Issues – Ensure your payment method is valid and has sufficient funds. Try using a different payment option if the issue persists.
  • Technical Glitches – Clear your browser cache, try a different browser, or refresh the page before attempting again.
  • Out of Stock Items – Some products may be temporarily unavailable. Check if the item is in stock or contact us for availability.
  • Incorrect Shipping Details – Double-check that your address and contact information are entered correctly.
  • Account/Login Issues – If required, make sure you’re logged into your account. Reset your password if you're unable to access your account.
  • Website Maintenance – If our website is under maintenance, try again later or reach out to our support team.
  • If you're still experiencing issues, please contact our support team at info@usodeals.com, and we’ll be happy to assist you.

How to change quantities of orders in the cart?

To update the quantity of an item in your cart, follow these steps:

  • Go to Your Cart – Click on the cart icon at the top of the page to view your selected items.
  • Find the Item – Locate the product you want to adjust.
  • Update the Quantity – Use the plus (+) or minus (-) buttons or manually enter the desired quantity in the input box.
  • Save or Refresh – Some carts update automatically, while others may require you to click an "Update" or "Apply Changes" button.
  • Check Stock Availability – If you can’t increase the quantity, the item may have a stock limit.
  • If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at info@usodeals.com for assistance.
